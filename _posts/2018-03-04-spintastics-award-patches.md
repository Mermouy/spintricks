<h3 style="text-align: center;">The Freestyle Spintastic Patch:</h3>
<p style="text-align: center;">To claim this patch you need to:</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Perform continuously for one minute on one throw of the top, utilizing a minimum of six tricks, including two different regeneration tricks.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">The ball-bearing top may NOT be used for this award.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">This is a good starting point to start cronstructing your own <a href="/category/watching/freestyles/">Spin Top Freestyle</a>!</p>
